Why Now

M6

Decision journal contains sequence patterns. Value function calibrated to attribution. The system knows which action sequences compound value. No stateless agent can match this.
Y1
Year 1
The memory is the foundation. A competitor can replicate the reasoning engine in a weekend. They cannot replicate 12 months of compounding institutional learning.
The switching cost isn't the software -- it's the loss of everything the system has learned. Stop the loop for 3-6 months while you re-platform, and your decisions degrade to static rules. That's the real cost of waiting.
